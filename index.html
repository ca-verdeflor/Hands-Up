Our Purpose

48% of Americans view gun violence as a big problem in our nation, and 53% of citizens prefer stricter gun laws. With this in mind, the implementation of gun violence prevention policies and/or laws is essential to protect the wellbeing of the public. Through raising awareness and providing methods, we hope to better the future of the American society.
