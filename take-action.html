Take Action

How?

Donations allow us to help families affected by gun violence, support gun violence prevention policies, and get involved in many other services and projects that will allow us to accomplish our mission.

Write a letter to your state senator on the importance of gun violence prevention and what it means to you. Also, be sure to check that you are registered to vote so that you can support leaders that aid our cause.
