About Hands Up

For Who?

This serves as a voice for individuals where gun violence is prevalent. These include proverty concentrated and underserved communities, POC (People of Color) communities (more specifically Black and Latine), schools, and the public as a whole. With the resources and support provided, I hope to garner the attention of lawmakers, gun owners, gun affiliated organizations (i.e. NRA), and anyone willing to listen.

Why?

As the frequency of gun violence become more constant, the more it becomes normalized. This only instills fear and panic to society, and I want to make sure that future generations will be able to feel safe and comfortable in public settings. For this reason, I decided to develop a website to spread awareness about the cause and compile resources where people can actively go in order to educate themselves and show support.

Learn More

Here is a list of sources you can use to better inform yourself about gun violence. Share them to your friends, family, or whomever you choose!


• UC Davis Health What Can You Do Contains information about firearm injury, as well as awareness and facts surrounding it.
• Gun Violence Archive Contains statistics about deaths and injuries as a result of various causes of gun violence since 2013.
• Amnesty International Global movement that contains information on various causes, one being gun violence. Key facts and ways to take action are provided.
